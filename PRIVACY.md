# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **YukinekoPlayer** ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**
We believe that your media viewing habits are private. We do not track your usage, playback history, or analyze your media files.

## 2. Local Processing
All media processing (video decoding, audio rendering, plugin effects) is performed locally on your device (macOS). Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.

## 3. In-App Purchases
Our app offers "Pro Mode" via In-App Purchases. All transactions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**最終更新日:** 2025年12月3日

## 1. データの収集について
**当方は、ユーザーの個人情報を一切収集・保存・共有しません。**
